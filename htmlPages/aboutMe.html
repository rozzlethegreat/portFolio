Hi, my name is Rosalind Cervante and I'm a web designer/developer based in Wellington, New Zealand.

I have studied graphic design for one year at a Dutch art college called Koninklijke Academie van Beeldende Kunsten (KABK), and upon returning to New Zealand picked up graphic design at Massey Wellington for another year, after doing a course in Python while there, I fell in love with tech and I left Massey for Yoobee School of Design to focus on web based design where I earned a Diploma in UX Design and Web Development, I have also earned Diploma in Software Development from Whitecliffe College. I have worked as a UX Designer and Front-End Web Developer at various companies including BNZ, Flinch Marketing and Xequals.

I'm currently Xequals Lead UX Designer, Xequals are a charming group of humans and drupal enthusiasts! Xequals.
